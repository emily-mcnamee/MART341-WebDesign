Brief Explanations to my website ideas

Video Game Reviews

I really enjoy going through video game sites and reading what people think of the releases, and making my own would be good practice on coding multiple
visual designs into one webpage. It also be good practice in linking multiple pages together, creating cohesion in the graphics, and finding out my preferred
color pallets. I think it would also be fun to write out my personal experiences with the game for others to read and make their own decisions about the game

Art Porfolio

As I already have a large backlog of past digital projects, it would be good practice to learn how to create a personalized website for my projects.
Having all of my projects in one place along with descriptions of the works would be helpful for when I'm submitting portfolios to jobs.
I can focus on page design and making sure everything fits well onto the page, creating a gallery feel to the webpage

Landscape/Indoor Plant Blog

Indoor plant care is one of my hobbies, so I'd a place to record everything I've grown and what problems I encountered.
I think it would be fun to include photos of the succulent pots I've grown, with listings of what's growing in the pot and specific care instructions.
There's a lot of gardening blogs out there but mainly focus on outdoor plant care. I like the idea of a website that has a "cottagecore" theme to it but specifially for indoor plant care.
